    <ul class="doc-list">

    <p>Your email address is used exclusively for:</p>
    <ul class="doc-list">
      <li>Notifying you when Omnigenius launches</li>
      <li>Sending early access invitations</li>
      <li>Occasional product updates and announcements you opted in for by joining the waitlist</li>
    </ul>
    <p>We will never sell, rent, or trade your email address to any third party for marketing purposes. We will never send you spam. Every communication we send includes a one-click unsubscribe link.</p>
  </div>

  <div class="doc-section">

    <p>Your data is stored securely using <strong>Supabase</strong>, a SOC 2 Type II certified database platform hosted on AWS infrastructure. Data is encrypted at rest and in transit using industry-standard TLS encryption.</p>
    <div class="highlight-box">
      <strong>We take security seriously.</strong> Access to our database is restricted to authorized personnel only. We do not store passwords or payment details on our waitlist platform.
    </div>
    <p>We retain your email address until you request removal, or until 90 days after the Omnigenius product reaches general availability — at which point we will migrate active subscribers to our main platform or delete inactive records.</p>
  </div>
